•for median
    
    •for odd observations

        122, 23, 74, 939 , 10000

        write in assending or descending order:

            23, 74, 122, 939, 10000

        total number of observations = 5 (odd)

        median = (n+1) / 2

        median = (5+1)/2
               = 3^rd number

        ∞ median = 122



    • for even observations
        
        12, 54, 23, 67, 87, 22

        write in assending or descending order:

            12, 22, 23, 54, 67, 87

        total number of observations = 6 (even)

        median = (n)/2 & (n/2)+1

        median = (n)/2
               = 6/2
               = 3 rd observations
               
               = (n+1)/2
               = (6/2)+1
               = 4 th observations

        ∞ median = (23+54)/2
                 = 38.5
